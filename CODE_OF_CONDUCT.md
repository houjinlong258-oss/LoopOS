# Code of Conduct

LoopOS contributors must communicate professionally, discuss technical decisions on their merits,
and provide a harassment-free environment regardless of identity, background, experience, or role.

Unacceptable conduct includes harassment, threats, discrimination, deliberate disclosure of private
information, sustained disruption, and misuse of project infrastructure. Maintainers may edit or
remove content, restrict participation, or remove access when necessary to protect the community.

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainers through the repository host. Reports will be
handled with discretion and conflicts of interest will be reassigned.
